Banana Pancakes Recipe

Description

Turn your overripe bananas into banana pancakes with just three ingredients. This recipe is flexibleâ€”make it gluten-free or adjust the servings based on how many bananas are available.

  • Prep Time 5 mins
  • Cook Time 20 mins
  • Total Time 25 mins

  • Servings 2 to 4 servings

Ingredients

For the pancakes

  • 4 overripe bananas
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 8 teaspoons butter, divided, for greasing the pan

Optional toppings

  • Fresh fruit
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Maple syrup
  • Salted butter
  • Whipped cream
